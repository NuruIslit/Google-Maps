GOOGLE MAPS STORY

Lisa and Shekinah were to embark on a journey to the same location, but from different start points. This meant they had to rely on Google maps.
And so Lisa decided to fully trust the assigned map to her. Shekinah though didn't. It so happened that Lisa got to her location in good time.
Shekinah took longer than her assigned time because she thought she knew better at some points and therefore didn't trust her assigned map.
Both would miss a turn at times and the map recalculated so that they were still on track. It was just a matter of trusting the map.
In the end Lisa reached to the destinantion and Shekinah didn't. Why? Cause Lisa knew better than to trust the assigned map while Shekinah trusted herself more.

THE LESSON

Proverbs 3:5-6
Trust in the LORD with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding. In all your ways acknowledge Him and He shall direct your paths.

Lisa trusted the assigned maps to her fully. Sometimes Google can mislead you but the Lord won't.
Even when Lisa and Shekinah got lost the map assigned thema new route to their destination. And so it is with God.
He has assigned you a path to your destiny and whenever you slip and miss a road a new one opens and still leads you to your destiny.
Trust Him wholly. Do not doubt and think you know better as Shekinah did. She ended up not making it.Lisa did.
Which character are you in your life? Shekinah or Lisa?
